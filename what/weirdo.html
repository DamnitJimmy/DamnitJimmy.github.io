Bruno Bustamente was a survivor of the Knox Infection, a virus of unknown origin, and a very bad driver, even though he committed at least 14 counts of grand theft auto. It honestly was a wonder he survived a whole month, before he finally wrecked a stolen cargo van full of items, that presumably were also stolen, into a building.

Fun Facts?

  • liked using a baseball bat. like A LOT
